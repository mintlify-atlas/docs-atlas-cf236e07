The Quejas Ciudadanas citizen portal is your gateway to reporting and tracking infrastructure problems in your community. This guide will walk you through the registration and login process.

## Accessing the Portal

When you first visit the Quejas Ciudadanas platform, you'll see the login page with two options:
- **Sign In** - For existing users
- **Register** - For new users

## Creating a New Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click the Register button">
    On the home page, click the "Registrarte" (Register) button to switch to the registration form.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Provide your identification">
    Select your identification type from the dropdown menu and enter your identification number.
    
    Available ID types include:
    - Cédula de Ciudadanía
    - Cédula de Extranjería
    - Pasaporte
    - And more
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter personal information">
    Fill in the required fields:
    - First name (Nombres)
    - Last name (Apellidos)
    - Email address (Correo electrónico)
    - Password (Contraseña)
    - Date of birth (Fecha de nacimiento)
    - Gender (Sexo)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select your user type">
    Choose your account type:
    - **Ciudadano** (Citizen) - For community members reporting issues
    - **Funcionario** (Agent) - For government officials responding to cases
    
    <Note>
      Most users should select "Ciudadano". The "Funcionario" option is for authorized government personnel only.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit your registration">
    Click the "Registrarte" button to create your account. If successful, you'll be automatically logged in and redirected to the reports dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Logging In

For returning users, the login process is straightforward:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter your credentials">
    On the login form, enter:
    - Your identification number (Número de identificación)
    - Your password (Contraseña)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Access">
    Click the "Acceder" (Access) button to log in. You'll be redirected to the reports dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  If you enter incorrect credentials, you'll see an error message. Double-check your identification number and password before trying again.
</Warning>

## What Happens After Login

Once you successfully log in, the system creates a session that stores:
- Your user ID and type
- Your personal information (name, email, ID)
- Your demographic details

This session allows you to:
- Create new infrastructure reports
- View existing reports on the map
- Track the status of your submissions

## Session Management

Your session remains active while you use the platform. When you're done:

1. Click the logout button in the navigation menu
2. Your session will be cleared and you'll return to the login page

<Tip>
  For security, always log out when using a shared or public computer.
</Tip>
